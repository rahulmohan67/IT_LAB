Trivandrum

Thiruvananthapuram (or Trivandrum) is the capital of the southern Indian state of Kerala.

It is the most populous city in Kerala with a population of 957,730 as of 2011

Referred to by Mahatma Gandhi as the "Evergreen city of India",the city is characterised by its undulating terrain of low coastal hills.
